Detailed Description

Aligns the robot with a black line by backing up until both line sensors detect alignment.

This class is typically used at a black line junction to help the robot square its rear side. It supports both standard (white-to-black transition) and inverted (black-to-white transition) alignment modes.

Requires:

  • A LineManager with calibrated line sensors ("lf-left" and "lf-right")
  • A MotorController for backward movement

Executes one iteration of the line squaring process.
